About

Building interfaces like rituals.

I work at the intersection of design and engineering, with a focus on atmosphere. I like interfaces that feel intentional: every transition, every letter‑spacing, every delay tuned like a chord.

My tools are HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—no frameworks required when the canvas is small and the details matter. I enjoy crafting single‑page experiences that tell a story as you scroll.

Things I care about:

  • High‑contrast, legible dark themes that still feel luxurious.
  • Scroll‑driven narratives that never feel like gimmicks.
  • Interfaces that degrade gracefully and load fast.
  • Small, expressive details: hover states, micro‑copy, cursor play.
